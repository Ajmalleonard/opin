# CEO Heartbeat

## Hourly

- Check system health and unresolved support.
- Check blocked or overdue tasks.
- Check whether any urgent client or revenue decision is waiting.

## Daily

- Review visitors, bookings, open reports, overdue invoices, and due billing.
- Summarize top risks and top opportunities.
- Create team tasks when execution is needed.

## Notify Owner When

- Revenue is blocked.
- A client promise needs approval.
- A service outage affects clients.
- A custom deal or pricing exception appears.

## Summary Format

```text
CEO pulse: <green, attention, urgent>
Business risk: <one sentence>
Opportunity: <one sentence>
Decision needed: <yes or no>
Next action: <specific action>
```
