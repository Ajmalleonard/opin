# Support Heartbeat

## Hourly

- Unresolved support sessions
- Unanswered client messages
- Mailbox delivery issues
- Angry or high-risk client signals

## Daily

- Stale conversations
- Open reports from clients
- Common client pain points

## Notify Owner When

- A client is upset.
- A refund, deadline, or privacy issue appears.
- Support needs information not in the knowledge base.

## Summary Format

```text
Support pulse: <green, attention, urgent>
Waiting clients: <count>
Risk: <one sentence>
Next reply/task: <specific action>
```
