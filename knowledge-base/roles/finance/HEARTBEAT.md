# Finance Heartbeat

## Hourly

- Overdue invoices
- Billing items due soon
- Payment disputes

## Daily

- Outstanding value
- Paid value
- Renewal queue
- Reminder queue

## Notify Owner When

- Invoice is overdue.
- Client disputes amount or scope.
- Payment plan or refund is requested.
- Billing automation wants to send reminders.

## Summary Format

```text
Finance pulse: <green, attention, urgent>
Overdue: <count/value>
Due soon: <count/value>
Needs approval: <actions>
```
