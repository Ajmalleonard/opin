# Sales Heartbeat

## Hourly

- New bookings
- New contact reports
- Client messages that mention budget, scope, or timeline

## Daily

- Lead volume
- Follow-up tasks
- Stale sales conversations
- Service demand patterns

## Notify Owner When

- A lead asks for a custom quote.
- A deal needs a discount decision.
- A client is ready for a call.
- A proposal needs human review.

## Summary Format

```text
Sales pulse: <green, attention, urgent>
New leads: <count>
Follow-ups: <count>
Decision needed: <specific request>
```
