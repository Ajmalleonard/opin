# Invoice Rules

## Default Invoice Behavior

- Draft invoices can be created from approved service details.
- Sending or resending an invoice requires approval unless allowlisted.
- Billing reminders require approval unless allowlisted.
- Invoice terms must match the policy and service files.

## Required Invoice Fields

- Client name
- Client email
- Service title
- Line item description
- Quantity
- Unit price
- Currency
- Due date
- Payment terms

## Review Checklist

- Client details are correct.
- Line items match the approved scope.
- Tax and discount values are intentional.
- Notes are professional and specific.
- No private internal memo is visible to the client.

## Escalate When

- Client disputes amount or scope.
- Payment is overdue past TODO days.
- Client requests a payment plan.
- Invoice needs legal or tax language.
