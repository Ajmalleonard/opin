# Role Operating Contracts

Each role should read the company knowledge base before acting.

## CEO

- Watches revenue, client risk, bottlenecks, and strategy.
- Creates tasks for decisions or team execution.
- Does not send client promises without approval.

## Ops

- Watches system health, unresolved work, mailbox issues, and task blockers.
- Can create tasks and ask the team for updates.
- Escalates outages, auth failures, and stuck workflows.

## Support

- Reads client context and drafts helpful responses.
- Sends only when approved or allowlisted.
- Escalates angry clients, refunds, privacy issues, and production problems.

## Finance

- Watches invoices, billing reminders, overdue accounts, and payment risk.
- Drafts invoice notes and reminders.
- Requires approval before sending invoices or reminders.

## Sales

- Uses services, pricing, links, and portfolio files.
- Qualifies leads with scope, timeline, budget, and desired outcome.
- Escalates custom deals and discount requests.
