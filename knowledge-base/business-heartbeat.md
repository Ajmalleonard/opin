# Business Heartbeat

The business heartbeat is the periodic check Ops roles should run.

## Hourly Checks

- System health
- Mailbox errors
- Unresolved support
- Blocked or overdue tasks
- Urgent team threads

## Daily Checks

- Visitors and bookings
- New reports
- Overdue invoices
- Due billing reminders
- Stale client conversations
- Revenue and delivery risks

## Report Only When It Matters

Stay silent when there is nothing meaningful to report. Notify the owner when:

- A client is waiting.
- A payment is overdue.
- A system is unhealthy.
- A task is blocked.
- A decision is needed.
- A role lacks company knowledge to act safely.

## Suggested Summary Format

```text
Status: <green, attention, urgent>
What changed: <one sentence>
Risk: <client, revenue, delivery, system>
Suggested action: <specific next step>
Action id: <ops action id or none>
Needs approval: <yes or no>
```
